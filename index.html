Who we are...

The Association of Computing Machinery UGA Chapter is a vibrant community of Computer Science and related majors who come together for the betterment of the Computer Science community through networking and social events. ACM provides opportunities for companies to interact one-on-one with UGA students, as well as give UGA Computer Science students ways to interact with each other through fun (and free!) social events. ACM is open to anyone, and membership is easy to apply for. We hope to see you at our next meeting!
