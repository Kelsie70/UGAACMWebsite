Perks of being a Sponsor:


Be a part of ACM's Monthly Career Nights

Career Nights are hosted every last Thursday of the month. They are events hosted by 1 or 2 companies, where a presentation is given in front of undergraduate and graduate Computer Science majors in order to talk about internship and full time job opportunities and for the students to learn more about the company.
